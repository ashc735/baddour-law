Areas of Practice

Criminal Law

Have you been accused of committing a crime? Our team at Baddour Law will help make sure you're treated fairly under the law, explains your options, and stands by your side through a stressful and complicated process. Whether you're innocent or made a mistake, having the right attorney can make a big difference.

Civil Litigation

Our team helps you handle legal disputes where money, rights, or property are at stake. Whether you're being sued or need to take legal action against someone else, we can guide you through the process, protect your interests, and work toward a resolution—either through negotiation or in court.

Injury & Wrongful Death

If you or a loved one has been seriously injured—or worse—because of someone else's negligence, recklessness, or a defective product, you may have a legal claim. Baddour Law helps clients pursue justice and compensation in cases involving wrongful death, car and truck accidents, dangerous products, and more. We handle these matters in both State and Federal Court, working to hold the responsible party accountable. If you've experienced a serious injury or loss, it's important to speak with an experienced trial attorney who can protect your rights and fight for the outcome you deserve.

Estate Planning & Probate

Baddour Law helps individuals plan for the future and manage what happens to their property after they pass away. This includes creating wills, trusts, powers of attorney, and healthcare directives to ensure your wishes are followed and your loved ones are protected. After someone passes, we can guide families through the legal process of settling the estate—making sure debts are paid and assets are properly distributed. These services help avoid confusion, reduce family conflict, and make a difficult time a little easier.

Trust & Estate Litigation

Trust and estate litigation involves resolving disputes over wills, trusts, and the distribution of a loved one's estate. These cases often arise when family members disagree about the validity of a will, how a trust is being managed, or whether someone acted unfairly or unlawfully. Baddour Law can represent you in court to protect your rights and ensure your loved one's wishes are honored. Trust and estate litigation helps bring clarity and resolution to complex, often emotional family matters.

Immigration Law

Our team helps individuals and families navigate the complex U.S. immigration system. This includes applying for visas, green cards, citizenship, and asylum, as well as defending clients in deportation or removal proceedings. We also provide assistance with family-based petitions, work permits, and other matters related to immigration status. Our goal is to make the process easier and help avoid costly mistakes.

Nuestro equipo ayuda a personas y familias a navegar el complejo sistema de inmigración de los Estados Unidos. Esto incluye la solicitud de visas, tarjetas de residencia (green cards), ciudadanía y asilo, así como la defensa de clientes en procesos de deportación o expulsión. También brindamos asistencia con peticiones familiares, permisos de trabajo y otros asuntos relacionados con el estatus migratorio. Nuestro objetivo es hacer que el proceso sea más sencillo y ayudar a evitar errores costosos.
